2.Death Note

As is the case with several anime titles on this list, Death Note is an iconic and highly influential series that is
perfect for beginners due it compelling and closed story. Death Note follows Yagami Light,
a high school student who discovers a Death Note at this school. This book gives Yagami the power to kill anyone
whose name he writes in the book, with Yagami using his power to enact vigilante justice.
